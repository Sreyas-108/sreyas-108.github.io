I am currently working as Member of Technical Staff 1 (MTS-1) at [Nutanix (Bangalore)](https://www.nutanix.com/). I have a BE in Computer Science from [BITS Pilani](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/) (graduated in May 2021). Primarily my interests are in the fields of Software Developement and Computer Vision.

Previously, I have contributed at [HBCSE-TIFR](https://www.hbcse.tifr.res.in/), [GSoC](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/) (2019 student), [GCIn](https://codein.withgoogle.com/archive/) (2019 mentor) and [Amazon](https://www.amazon.in/). I have conducted research collaborations at [ADAPT lab](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/pilani/computerscience/AdvancedDataAnalyticsParallelTechnologiesLaboratory), [Multimedia lab](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/pilani/computerscience/MultimediaHCILaboratory) and [VAL lab (IISc)](https://val.cds.iisc.ac.in/index.html). Recently, I have collaborated with Dr. Joost and Dr. Marc at [LAMP (Computer Vision Center, Barcelona)](http://www.cvc.uab.es/lamp/), where I worked on interpretations of catastrophic forgetting in a task incremental setup from robustness perspective.

On the personal front, I am avidly interested in travel, photography, swimming and playing keyboard. Feel free to connect with me over [email](mailto:sreyasravichandran@gmail.com)!
